# 林夏-五阶段造型-提示词

## 用途

- 角色：林夏
- 目标：五阶段造型设定 sheet
- 阶段：基于已确定脸部锚点，延展不同剧情阶段的服装与状态版本
- 用途说明：用于建立林夏在不同剧情推进中的稳定造型变化，保证“同一张脸 + 不同阶段处境”

## 原始提示词

```text
Character costume evolution sheet for Lin Xia from the Chinese cyberpunk philosophical drama 'The Edge of Consciousness', using the same face identity across all versions. Adult Chinese woman, late 20s, lean build, restrained and observant expression, sharp focused eyes, cool intelligent presence, subtle emotional pressure, stable facial identity suitable for long-form comic continuity. Visual style: restrained cinematic realism, polished AI comic concept art, clean linework, soft but precise rendering, neutral gray design-sheet background.

Show five full-body character designs of the same woman on one sheet, each clearly labeled as a different story phase:
1. Federation consciousness sculptor phase: clean, precise, low-noise futuristic professional attire, cool white and gray-blue tones.
2. Escape transition phase: retains federation structure but adds practical outerwear, slight disorder, beginning contact with harsh reality.
3. Real-world action phase: more functional layered clothing for movement, infiltration and survival, muted industrial tones, light wear, but still neat and intelligent.
4. Rift/core action phase: simplified, focused, streamlined silhouette, less decorative, more concentrated and emotionally hardened.
5. Bridge guardian phase: balanced middle-ground aesthetic between physical reality and digital order, stable, understated, durable, no authority costume, no queen-like styling.

Keep hairstyle consistent and practical across all five versions. No glamor fashion, no wasteland armor, no exaggerated action poses, no fantasy accessories. Emphasize the same person evolving through environment and responsibility rather than becoming a different archetype.
```

- 人物连续性：same face identity, stable facial identity, long-form comic continuity
- 人物气质：restrained, observant, sharp focused eyes, cool intelligent presence, subtle emotional pressure
- 视觉方向：restrained cinematic realism, polished AI comic concept art
- 输出形式：five full-body character designs on one sheet
- 阶段逻辑：

  - 逃离过渡期
  - 现实行动期
